Movies

(Upper panel)  Heat map movie showing expression, methylome and H3K4me3 data from 812 genes on days 1,3 and 5 after Esrrb downregulation. (Lower panel) Heat map movie showing expression and proteome data from 695 genes on days 1,3 and 5 after Esrrb downregulation. Data is projected onto two-dimensional hexagonal arrays. Individual hexagons representing specific genes or gene-products are dynamically coloured according to the fold changes in each of the three or two molecular layers. The positions of each gene or its product within the hexagon array are maintained across the layers and time points. This dynamic data representation of the Waddington landscape facilitates genome-wide and temporal comparisons across the different molecular layers, and allows clustering of genes with similar dynamics across multiple gene expression regulatory layers.

These flash movies were created using GATE
