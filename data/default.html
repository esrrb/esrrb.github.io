Data for Download

We report data from five molecular regulatory layers at four time points Day0, 1, 3, and 5 after Esrrb knockdown and the subsequence analysis as a result of its integration. Specifically, we studied:

Transcriptome - mRNA Microarrays: contains files related to gene expression microarray data to quantify mRNA abundance (designated as RNA)

Nuclear Proteome: contains files related to protein mass spectrometry data to measure nuclear protein abundance (designated as PRO)

Epigenome: Histone ChIP-Seq: contains files related to genome wide patterns of H3K4me3 and H3K27me3 and H3 through specific chromatin-immunoprecipitation assay coupled with massive parallel DNA sequencing (Illumina GA IIx and HiSeq 2000 platform) to asses chromatin modifications over time (designated as H3K4me3, H3K27me3 and H3).

Methylome: Contains files related to methylation data using HELP Assay (HpaII tiny fragment Enrichment by Ligation-mediated PCR (Khulan et al., 2006) to study changes in the promoter methylation pattern.

miRNA Expression Profile: contains microRNA expression profile over the time series from two different platforms: miRNA-Seq (Illumina GA IIx and HiSeq 2000 platform) or microRNA array (Exiqon, Denmark) to explain possible discordances in the mRNA and protein levels related with microRNAs.

Esrrb ChIP-Seq: contains files with the specific binding sites for Esrrb to elucidate Esrrb direct gene and miRNA targets.
