Methylome

Supplementary  Data 3. Shows normalized methylome data through HELP Assay based on (HpaII Tiny Fragment (HTF) Enrichment by Ligation mediated PCR) (Bird, 1986) from three biological replicates over days 0,1,3 and 5 after Esrrb knockdown. This data represents levels in the methylome pattern across the genome as a function of time.

Supplementary  Data 14. Wiggle Files (.WIG - format description) zipped for visualization of Esrrb specific binding sites through the entire mouse genome using the University of California Santa Cruz (UCSC-genome browser) http://genome.ucsc.edu/cgi-bin/hgGateway. To upload the files, browse to http://genome.ucsc.edu/cgi-bin/hgCustom for mouse. In the “Paste URLs or Data” section, select “Browse…” on the right of the screen. Use the pop-up window to select the copied files, and then select “Submit”. The upload process may take some time.
