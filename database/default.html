Relational Database

We have extracted the information from supplementary data 12 to provide an interactive database of the information. This table contains integrated, normalized data from all the regulatory layers analyzed in our experiment. The data from days 0, 1, 3, and 5 from all layers is given. E represents expression data, P is proteome data, M is methylome data, and K4 and K27 are histone expression data.
