Figure S6 Esrrb regulated mRNA/miR feed-forward motifs.
(A) Schematic view of the Esrrb mRNA/miR feed-forward motif network where miR-target interactions are predicted from TargetScan. (B-D) Representation of days 1, 3 and 5 post-Esrrb depletion networks where Esrrb ChIP-seq binding targets, mRNA expression changes (circles), miR expression changes (triangles) and TargetScan 5.1 predictions have been combined to search for feed-forward motifs at all three time points with Esrrb as the root regulatory node. Nodes are colored with a gradient ranging from dark blue characterizing maximum negative log2 fold change to bright orange for maximum positive log2 fold change relative to day 0 expression levels. All three subnetworks point to mmu-mir-182, mmu-mir-183, mmu-mir-762, , mmu-mir-106b and mmu-mir-291a-5p as key Esrrb-regulated target genes. Dotted hammerhead edges connect miRs to one or more of their predicted target mRNAs. (E) Schematic view of the Esrrb mRNA/miR feed-forward motif network where miR-target interactions were determined experimentally and are listed on miRTarBase and/or miRecords. (F-H) Representations of day 1, 3 and 5 post-Esrrb depletion networks as described above but with experimentally validated miR-target interactions. Overlapping miR-target interaction with the predicted miR-target networks (B-D) are highlighted in thick solid blue lines. (G) Observed occurrences of different kinds of Esrrb-miR motifs according to time points (dark grey) and their null distributed expected counts derived from network randomization by edge shuffling (light grey) for the networks visualized in (F-H).
